Skiing Around the World

Skiing offers not just the thrill of speed and snow, but also access to some of the world’s most stunning mountain landscapes. From alpine resorts to off-piste backcountry, here are my personal highlights and tips.

Top Destinations

  • Japan (Niseko, Hakuba): Powder heaven with great food.
  • France (Chamonix, Val Thorens): Classic Alps with dramatic terrain.
  • Canada (Whistler): Vast ski area with reliable snow.
  • Chile (Portillo): South American slopes during the northern summer.

Recommended Gear

  • All-mountain skis (light and versatile)
  • Helmet & goggles (anti-fog lenses)
  • Merino wool base layers
  • Avalanche gear (if going off-piste): beacon, shovel, probe
  • Backpack with hydration system

Travel Tips

  • Flights: Use Skyscanner or Google Flights to monitor deals. Aim for midweek travel.
  • Accommodation: Look for ski-in/ski-out hostels or Airbnb near the slopes for value.
  • Passes: Multi-resort passes like Ikon or Epic can save money if you're visiting several places.
  • Timing: Shoulder season (early Dec or late March) offers lower prices and fewer crowds.
