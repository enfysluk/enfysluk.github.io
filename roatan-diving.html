ü§ø Recommended Diving and Snorkeling Gear

  • Mask & Snorkel: Choose a comfortable, well-fitting mask with tempered glass and a purge valve snorkel for easy clearing.
  • Fins: Opt for lightweight, durable fins; split fins or paddle fins depending on your style.
  • Wetsuit: Thickness depends on water temperature; 3mm for tropical, 5mm or more for cooler waters.
  • Dive Computer: Essential for monitoring depth, time, and decompression limits safely.
  • BCD & Regulator: Invest in reliable buoyancy control devices and regulators for comfortable and safe dives.
  • Underwater Camera: Capture your memories with waterproof housings or action cameras.
  • Safety Gear: Surface marker buoy (SMB), dive knife, and whistle enhance safety during dives.
